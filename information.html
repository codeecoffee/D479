Currency & Payments

  • US Dollar widely accepted
  • Major credit cards welcome
  • ATMs in tourist areas
  • Banks open Mon-Fri, 9AM-4PM

Local Customs

  • Modest dress at temples
  • Remove shoes when invited to homes
  • Ask before taking photos
  • Respect quiet hours (10PM-7AM)

Business Hours

  • Shops: 9AM-9PM
  • Restaurants: 7AM-11PM
  • No alcohol sales: 12AM-9AM
  • Tourist Office: 8AM-6PM

Important Guidelines

Language

English widely spoken in tourist areas. Local language predominant in rural regions.

Power

120V, same as US. Standard US-style outlets available.

Safety

Low crime rate but take standard precautions. Keep valuables in hotel safe.
